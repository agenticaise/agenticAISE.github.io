Replication of the literature review

This page documents the Scopus search strategy, screening steps, and data files used to identify secondary studies on Agentic AI in Software Engineering.

Search protocol

Scopus query

Source: Scopus. Search scope: metadata + entire paper.

Search string

( "agentic AI" OR "AI agents" OR "autonomous agents" OR "software agents" )
AND ( "software engineering" )
AND ( taxonomy OR survey OR "secondary study" OR review OR "mapping study" OR "empirical study" )

Exact exported Scopus query: searchString.pdf

Rationale & filters

  • Terminology coverage: we included multiple terms (agentic AI, AI agents, autonomous agents, software agents) to capture how the topic is discussed across SE literature.
  • Study types: we focused on synthesis-oriented evidence (taxonomies, surveys, reviews, mapping studies, and related secondary studies).
  • Time window: papers published after 2023.
  • Discipline filter: Computer Science / AI journals and conferences.

After filters, Scopus returned 233 papers (see CompleteList.csv).

Selection flow

From 233 to 11 studies

Step 1 — Initial retrieval

233 papers after filters → CompleteList.csv.

Step 2 — Title screening

First pruning by title → 28 papers → 28Selected.csv.

Step 3 — Full-text eligibility check

  • Include: secondary studies on Agentic AI in SE.
  • Exclude: papers not focused on SE activities/tasks.

Result: 7 papers → FinalList_scopus.csv.

Step 4 — Snowballing

Added 2 papers → FinalList_scopusPlusSnowballing.csv.

Step 5 — Targeted venue check (SE2030)

Checked ACM TOSEM “2030 Roadmap for Software Engineering” special issue (Vol. 34, Iss. 5, 2025); added 2 papers.

Final set: 11 papers → FinalList_scopusPlusSnowballingPlusSE2030.csv.

Files

Downloadable data

  • CompleteList.csv — Scopus results after filters (n=233).
  • 28Selected.csv — Title-screened subset (n=28).
  • FinalList_scopus.csv — Eligible set (n=7).
  • FinalList_scopusPlusSnowballing.csv — After snowballing (n=9).
  • FinalList_scopusPlusSnowballingPlusSE2030.csv — Final set (n=11).
  • searchString.pdf — Exported Scopus query (exact string).
