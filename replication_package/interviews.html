Replication of the expert interviews

To assess the plausibility, clarity, and relevance of the proposed taxonomy of Agentic AI in Software Engineering, we conducted an expert-based validation.

Study description

Expert-based validation

Given the conceptual and socio-technical nature of the contribution, the goal of this validation was not to empirically test predictive claims, but to critically examine whether the identified agent types, associated benefits, risks, and changes constitute a meaningful and useful conceptual framework from the perspective of domain experts.

The validation involved 6 experts with experience in software engineering and Agentic AI, including both academic researchers and industry practitioners. All participants had prior familiarity with AI-assisted or agent-based systems in software engineering contexts. Participation was voluntary; experts were informed that the validation focused on the conceptual soundness of the taxonomy rather than evaluating specific tools or implementations.
