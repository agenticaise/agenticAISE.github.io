Evidence grounding

Evidence sources across the three construction phases

The first iteration used a focused search strategy to identify secondary studies and related papers that could scope the domain. This produced an 11-paper scoping corpus after screening, snowballing, and complementary checks of recent SE2030-related literature. The list is available as FinalList_scopusPlusSnowballingPlusSE2030.csv.

The second and third iterations used a broader evidence base. In particular, the Tetrad construction required targeted literature on productivity, learning, empathy, collaboration, accountability, trust, deskilling, governance, and human expertise. These sources include primary empirical studies, conceptual papers, practitioner-oriented analyses, community surveys, industry reports, and workshop insights.

For traceability, we quantified the sources cited in the extended Tetrad construction. The long construction material cites 112 unique source items across the four Tetrad dimensions: 32 in Enhances, 28 in Obsolesces, 27 in Retrieves, and 62 in Reverses. These counts are not additive because a source can support more than one dimension. The corresponding source-by-source file is available as tetrad_source_traceability.csv.
