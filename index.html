Agentic AI in Software Engineering: Tools or Team Members?

Supporting material for the IEEE TSE manuscript. This website separates the description of the work, the final outcomes, and the additional construction and validation material used to build the taxonomy and analysis.

Scope of this website. This is not a second version of the paper. It is an online appendix: it documents how the work was constructed, reports the final taxonomy, and exposes supporting material for scrutiny and reuse. The manuscript PDF is intentionally not included for download here.

Evidence base

Evidence grounding across the construction phases

The first iteration produced an 11-paper scoping corpus used to define the initial scope of Agentic AI in Software Engineering. The Tetrad and the risk-benefit analysis were then grounded through a broader second-iteration construction process: targeted searches, backward and forward snowballing, primary empirical studies, conceptual papers, industry-oriented evidence, community surveys, SE2030 workshop insights, and expert elicitation.

The Extended Tetrad & literature page brings these materials together: it documents the first-iteration scoping files and the broader source base used for the McLuhan Tetrad construction.
